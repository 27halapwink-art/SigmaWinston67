History


Ichiraku Ramen is a small, family-run ramen stand founded by Teuchi in the Hidden Leaf Village, later joined by his daughter Ayame; built on simple food and genuine hospitality, the shop became known for welcoming anyone who walked through its curtains without judgment, which is why it became a special place for Naruto Uzumaki, offering him warmth, acceptance, and a sense of belonging long before he was recognized by the rest of the village.
